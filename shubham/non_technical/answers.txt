1. How do you stay updated about topics in Flutter/Dart and active in the community? (Discord/Slack/MeetUps/Twitter)
A) I have joined several Flutter communities on Discord and LinkedIn, and try best to be active there. Also I always check what Flutter have changed in new updates.

2. Your most used IDE/keyboard shortcuts when coding. (for Flutter/Dart/Typescript)
A) VScode is my most used IDE, for Flutter and Dart I mostly use Ctrl+Shift+R for Refactoring.

3. How do you approach responsiveness when writing code for an entire screen? 
A) I try to divide the whole screen into several widgets and only keep widgets stateful if they are needed, and possibly try to add animations wherever possible.
